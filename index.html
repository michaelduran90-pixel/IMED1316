The Moon Observation Center was created to bring people together under the shared wonder of the night sky. This website serves as your gateway to exploring the moon’s beauty, science, and history through guided sessions, community events, and interactive learning tools. Whether you're visiting for inspiration, scientific curiosity, or peaceful reflection, our platform provides an easy way to discover everything happening throughout the week. For the community, this site acts as a central hub where residents can join scheduled moon-viewing gatherings, attend workshops, and connect with local astronomers and storytellers. Each event is thoughtfully designed to encourage learning, conversation, and a deeper appreciation for the natural world. By offering regular sessions at scenic observation points around the area, we hope to create shared experiences that strengthen community bonds and spark curiosity in all ages. Students will find the website especially helpful as it offers structured learning opportunities aligned with real lunar cycles and astronomical phenomena. From guided telescope sessions to hands-on crater-mapping workshops, the activities listed on the schedule support both school programs and personal exploration. With sign-up tools, event descriptions, and educational resources all in one place, students can easily plan their visits, enhance their science studies, and build meaningful connections with mentors and peers who share their interest in space

Register for our free event

To make the most of your lunar experience, we invite all visitors to sign up using the website’s built-in form and schedule buttons. Registering helps us prepare the right equipment, coordinate group sizes, and keep you informed about any special events or rare celestial moments happening throughout the week. Simply click the schedule button beside the sessions you’re interested in or complete the sign-up form on the main page to reserve your spot. By joining officially, you’ll receive timely updates, guidance from our team, and a more personalized journey through each phase of the moon’s nightly transformation.
